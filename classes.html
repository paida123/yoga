Beginner Yoga

It is a great way to introduce yourself to the world of yoga. It focuses on building foundational poses, improving flexibility, and increasing strength at a comfortable pace.

Group Yoga

Group yoga is a wonderful way to deepen your practice while enjoying a sense of community and support.

Pregnancy Yoga

Pregnant yoga is a gentle and effective way to stay active, relieve discomfort, and prepare your body for childbirth.

Couples Yoga

It is a unique way for partners to connect, strengthen their bond, and enhance their communication.

Barre Yoga

It combines the fluidity and mindfulness of yoga with the strengthening and toning elements of barre exercises.

Bilkam

It is a dynamic style of yoga practiced in a heated room, typically set to 105°F (40°C).
