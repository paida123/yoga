About us

At Peaceful Play Yoga, we believe in the transformative power of yoga. Our studio is a sanctuary where individuals of all levels can explore mindfulness, flexibility, and inner peace. We offer a variety of yoga styles, from Hatha and Vinyasa to Restorative and Meditation-focused sessions. Our highly trained instructors are dedicated to guiding you on your journey towards well-being and self-discovery.... Founded by Auxilia, Peaceful Play Yoga was born out of a passion for holistic wellness and mindfulness. Our mission is to create a welcoming space where students feel empowered, connected, and at peace.
